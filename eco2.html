Top Attractions

Setouchi Festival

The festival of Setouchi is celebrated rarely, and all the people in the community share their thoughts and worship the gods together.

Okayama Castle

This is one of the best places to visit. The place is very clean and surrounded by the sea and green forest. It also has an olive oil forest and plants.

Ushimado

A beautiful sea where you can relax and enjoy the view. Perfect for swimming with family and friends during summer.

Culture & Food

Local Culture

Historical places that are great for visitors. They express the history of Setouchi and Okayama.

Art Festivals

Handmade art created by local people, very famous in Setouchi. Visit the Setouchi Triennale for contemporary art exhibitions.

Historic Temples

Explore the Okayama Castle which is very famous. Visitors can enjoy the castle's unique architecture.
